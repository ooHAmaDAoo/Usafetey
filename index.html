About Us

About Us

Usafety for Extension LPG Pipes is a company specialized in installing firefighting equipment systems, fire alarms, and mechanical ventilation.

Usafety is a leading company in installing firefighting and alarm systems, and providing mechanical ventilation solutions. Based in Kuwait, we adhere to the highest standards of quality and safety. We provide installation and maintenance services for advanced fire-fighting and ventilation systems. Our vision seeks to enhance safety and security in society, and achieve the effectiveness of our engineering systems.

Mission and Vision

At Usafety, our mission is providing advanced and effective solutions in the areas of installing firefighting systems and alarm devices, in addition to providing mechanical ventilation services, committed to the highest standards of quality and safety, to achieve customer satisfaction and enhance safety in society.

Our strategic vision is to be recognized leaders in the safety systems installation industry, by providing innovative engineering solutions and continuously improving the level of safety and quality, to be a reliable local and global company that contributes to building a safe and sustainable society.
