Company introduction

Usafety Company is considered a leader in the field of installing firefighting equipment systems, fire alarms, and mechanical ventilation. It provides advanced and effective solutions that comprehensively meet market needs. The company was established with the aim of achieving the highest standards of quality and safety in this vital field. Usafety Company is headquartered in Kuwait and operates in accordance with international standards approved by the Kuwait Fire Department. The company is unique in providing customized solutions to meet safety and security requirements, which makes it a reliable partner for many companies and institutions. Among Usafety's services is the installation and maintenance of comprehensive fire-fighting systems, which include modern fire alarms and checking them regularly to ensure their permanent effectiveness. The company also provides mechanical ventilation services with advanced designs that meet the requirements of the industrial and commercial environment. Usafety also carries out sanitation works with high precision, while adhering to strict environmental and health standards. The company studies and analyzes customer needs to provide unique and fully appropriate solutions for different projects. As a company committed to quality and sustainability, Usafety seeks to achieve its vision of improving the level of safety and security in society and enhancing the effectiveness of the engineering systems it provides.
